About the Art

Life Death/Knows Doesn't Know (1968)

Neon tubing with clear glass tubing suspension frames


This piece of work by Bruce Nauman plays off our expectations of neon signage. Nauman uses contradictory terms - life/death, pleasure/pain, cares/doesn't care - to confront us with our own positions between emotionally heavy opposites.
